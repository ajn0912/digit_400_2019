About

Welcome to Animal Network! The site was created by Alex Newcamp, a local resident of Erie, Pennsylvania. Alex is an animal owner and always wished there was a place that where he could easily locate local resources to help care for his pet. So that is what he set out to create. The goal of the site is to inform people on where they can find valuable resources to help them care for their pet. We provide pet owners with information as well as a way to contact services that would be useful to them. Here you will find pages on local Kennels, Animal Hospitals, Pet Training, and Pet Grooming! On those pages you will find links to different websites as well as phone numbers, emails, and addresses for the companies listed.

Email us with any quesions or comments at animalnetwork2019@gmail.com!
